# 📜 Community Rules — GameSeek

> *Damit es für alle fair, sicher und angenehm bleibt.*

These rules apply to all GameSeek platforms — the app, the website, and all community spaces.

-----

### 01 · 🤝 Respect & Fairness

Treat others the way you want to be treated.

Insults, hate speech, harassment, and discrimination of any kind are strictly prohibited and will not be tolerated.

-----

### 02 · 🚫 No Cheating & Exploits

Cheats, hacks, or the intentional abuse of bugs and exploits are not allowed.

Violations will result in an **immediate and permanent ban** — no exceptions.

-----

### 03 · 🔇 No Spam & Advertising

Unsolicited advertising, spam, or mass messaging is prohibited.

Keep conversations relevant and respectful to everyone in the community.

-----

### 04 · 🔒 Respect Privacy

Do not share private or personal data of other users without their explicit consent.

Everyone has a right to their privacy — respect it.

-----

### 05 · 🔞 No Inappropriate Content

Explicit, offensive, or adult-only content must not be shared on any GameSeek platform.

GameSeek is designed for all age groups and must remain a safe space for everyone.

-----

## ⚠️ Consequences

Violations of these rules may result in:

- ⚠️  A formal **warning**
- 🔇  A temporary **mute or restriction**
- 🔨  A temporary or permanent **ban**

The severity of the action depends on the nature and frequency of the violation.  
GameSeek reserves the right to take action at its own discretion.

-----

*These rules exist to keep GameSeek a fair, fun, and welcoming place for everyone.*  
*Thank you for being part of the community. 🎮*
