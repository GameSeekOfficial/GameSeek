# 🎮 Join the GameSeek Team

> **GameSeek** is on a mission to build a simple, modern, and beautiful platform for gamers — and we want *you* to be part of it.

We’re a small but passionate team, and every single person who joins makes a real difference.
Whether you lead, code, design, test, or build community — there’s a place for you here.

-----

## 🌟 Why Join?

- 🚀  Be part of something from the **very beginning**
- 🛠️  Have a **real impact** on the product and its direction
- 🤝  Work in a **friendly, open, and welcoming team**
- 🎯  Build something you can be **proud to show off**
- 📚  **Learn and grow** alongside the project

-----

## 🧩 Open Roles

Every role in GameSeek is valuable. Find the one that fits you best.

-----

### 👑 Project Lead

The heart of the project. You keep everything moving and help shape what GameSeek becomes.

- Make key decisions about the project’s direction and priorities
- Coordinate across all teams — Dev, Design, Community, and Social
- Represent GameSeek and its values both internally and externally
- Keep the big picture in mind while supporting every team

> This role carries real responsibility — and real influence over what GameSeek becomes.

-----

### 🔧 Developer Lead

Lead the development team and own technical decisions for your area.

- Coordinate developers and review their contributions
- Set the technical direction for your area — App, Website, Backend, or Frontend
- Keep the codebase clean, consistent, and always moving forward
- Mentor and support your developers

> Solid hands-on experience required. You should enjoy leading as much as building.

-----

### 🎨 Design Lead

Define the visual soul of GameSeek and lead the design team.

- Own design decisions for both the **App** and the **Website**
- Create and maintain a consistent design language across all platforms
- Review designs and give constructive feedback to other designers
- Work closely with developers to ensure designs come to life properly

> A strong eye for UI/UX and experience with mobile and web design is a big plus.

-----

### 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Community Lead

Build, shape, and lead the GameSeek community.

- Plan and organize **community projects and events**
- Manage and guide the community support team
- Maintain a positive, active, and welcoming space
- Be the bridge between the community and the core team

> You should be passionate about people and have a natural instinct for community building.

-----

### 🛠️ Developer

Help build GameSeek from the ground up. Choose the area that fits you best:

|Area          |Focus                                      |
|--------------|-------------------------------------------|
|🌐 **Website** |Build and improve the GameSeek web platform|
|📱 **App**     |Work on the GameSeek mobile app            |
|🔧 **Backend** |APIs, databases, and server-side logic     |
|🎨 **Frontend**|UI components, layouts, and user experience|

**Tech stack we work with:**

|                    |                         |
|--------------------|-------------------------|
|⚛️ React + TypeScript|Core frontend framework  |
|🟨 JavaScript        |General scripting & logic|
|🐍 Python            |Backend & tooling        |


> You don’t need to know everything — apply for what you’re confident in and enjoy!

-----

### 🖌️ Designer

Shape the look and feel of GameSeek across all platforms.

- Design UI screens and components for the **App** and **Website**
- Create layouts, icons, illustrations, and other visual assets
- Collaborate with the Design Lead and developers to ensure quality and consistency
- Help improve the overall user experience of GameSeek

> Tools like Figma or Adobe XD are a plus — but a strong creative eye matters most.

-----

### 📋 Project Support

Keep the project organized, structured, and on track.

- Collect and organize ideas, feedback, and suggestions from the team
- Help plan and structure tasks and workflows
- Support the Project Lead with coordination and planning
- Be the reliable backbone that holds things together behind the scenes

-----

### 📷 Social Media Manager

Grow GameSeek’s presence and reach the gaming world.

- Create engaging content for social platforms
- Produce videos, posts, and announcements
- Help build and strengthen GameSeek’s brand and reputation
- Work with the design and community team on campaigns and launches

-----

### 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Community Support

Be the friendly face of the GameSeek community.

- Welcome new members and answer their questions
- Keep the community active, positive, and fun
- Support the Community Lead with events and initiatives
- Report feedback and issues to the team

-----

### 🧪 Beta Tester

Be among the first to experience what’s coming to GameSeek — before anyone else.

**What you’ll do:**

- Test new features and builds before public release
- Report bugs, issues, and share honest feedback
- Help shape the final product with your perspective

**What you get:**

- 🎮 **Early access** to new versions and features before the public
- 👀 A behind-the-scenes look at how GameSeek is built
- 💬 Direct contact with the dev team — your feedback truly matters
- 🏅 Recognition as one of GameSeek’s earliest supporters

> No technical experience needed — just a sharp eye and honest feedback!
