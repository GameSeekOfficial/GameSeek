## 🙋 About

GameSeek is an independent project created and driven by a young solo developer at just 18 years old.

The vision is simple: build a **clean, modern, and intuitive platform** where gamers can connect, find teammates, and grow communities — without unnecessary complexity getting in the way.

Every line of code, every design decision, every feature — built independently with a clear purpose.

---

## 🎯 Vision

GameSeek is designed for players who just want things to *work* — no clutter, no confusion.

A space where gamers can:

* 🎮 **Connect** with other players easily
* 👥 **Find teammates** that match their playstyle
* 🌍 **Build and grow communities** without friction

The goal is a platform that feels fast, familiar, and genuinely useful from day one.

## 🔒 Security & Privacy

The safety of our community is a priority.

To protect GameSeek from spam, abuse, malicious activity, and other security threats, certain technical information such as IP addresses may be logged and processed by our security systems. These logs are used exclusively for platform protection, blacklist management, and automated moderation systems.

When contacting support, we may store your email address and the information you provide so we can respond to inquiries, manage support tickets, and assist users effectively.

We only collect information necessary to operate, secure, and improve GameSeek. User data is never sold, and information is handled responsibly and only for its intended purpose.

Our goal is to create a safe, transparent, and trustworthy environment where gamers can connect with confidence.
